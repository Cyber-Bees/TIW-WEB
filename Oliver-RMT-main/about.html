About Us

My name is Oliver Bee. I opened Toronto Island Wellness in 2023 because I felt that my community was in need of a space dedicated to meeting people's needs in the realm of physical & remedial wellness.

My goal with TIW is to provide wellness workshops, community programs, and remedial/preventive therapy to Toronto Island's Lakeshore community, along with creating opportunities for people to deepen their knowledge and understanding of how to best care for their bodies. We take an informed approach, understanding that you know your body better than anyone else and offering tools and services for you to work with and improve on what you already know.

Toronto Island Wellness is an ideal retreat from the hectic buzz of city life in Canada's most densely populated urban centre. We welcome you to take a break from the constant stress that is life in downtown Toronto, hop on the ferry, and make your way over for a centering massage. While you're here, we might suggest that you grab some food at the Riviera, play a round of disc golf at the local course, or to take some time to stroll around and soak up the beautiful scenery of Toronto Island's park system.

Peace and tranquility is only a boat ride away!
